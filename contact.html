Frequently Asked Questions

How are prints shipped?

Prints are professionally printed on archival-quality paper and shipped flat in protective packaging. Shipping times vary by location.

What's included with digital downloads?

Digital purchases include high-resolution files suitable for personal printing. A personal use license is included. Commercial use requires a separate license.

Can I request a photo that's not in the gallery?

I have an extensive archive beyond what's shown. Feel free to ask about specific subjects or locations.

Do you offer framing?

Currently prints are sold unframed to keep costs down and allow you to choose framing that matches your space.
