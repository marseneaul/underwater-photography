About

Max Arseneault

I'm a software engineer by trade and an underwater photographer by obsession. I picked up diving over seven years ago and quickly realized I needed a way to share what I was seeing down there. The camera came shortly after.

Since then I've logged over 400 dives across South Africa, the Maldives, Italy, the Caribbean, and the UK. Sharks are what I chase most - there's nothing like being eye-to-eye with an apex predator in its own world. But I'll point the lens at anything that moves underwater.

I shoot with a Sony A7R IV in a Nauticam housing, lit by dual Ikelite DS160 strobes. I'm a PADI Divemaster based in Miami, Florida.
